The 6 Pillars of SHPE

Professional Development

SHPE prepares our member to further their professional career through networking opportunities.

Leadership Development

SHPE enhances professionals' leadership abilities by exposing them to leadership roles and seminars.

Chapter Development

Support the chapter and help it continue to grow by creating a welcoming community.

Outreach and Community Service

Give back to the community through mentorship opportunities and by spreading STEM awareness and support to the Hispanic community.

Career Development

Activities geared towards the career and management development with the specific intent of upward mobility and/or advancement within the members' career

Student/Professional Chapter Interaction

Activities promoting professional and local student SHPE chapter interaction and mentorship by the SHPE professional chapter.
