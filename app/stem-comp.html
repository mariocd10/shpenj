About

The Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE) was formed in Los Angeles, California in 1974 by a group of Hispanic engineers to promote the advancement of Hispanics in engineering and science. The Society has the objective to serve as role models and to promote educational programs for minority youth. Currently, SHPE is composed of student and professional chapters with membership approaching ten thousand.

A crucial part of SHPE’s agenda is our involvement with our community, especially with pre-college students for whom we plan on hosting a yearly Case Study Competition. The program’s objective is to provide high school students the opportunity to develop and deliver a technical response towards a real-life topic; whilst promoting the pursuit of higher education in the science, engineering, technology, and math fields.
