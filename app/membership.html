How to Become a Member

  1. Navigate to the SHPE National Membership Page and click Join Now!

  2. Create an Account

  3. Login to your Profile Page and Click on Join/Renew Membership and select one of the membership packages available (ex: Professional).

  4. Select Region 4 and New Jersey for chapter region

  5. Sign up for our Mailing List for the latest news from us
