Scholarship

Description

SHPE NJ will be awarding a $500 scholarship to a worthy SHPE member. This SHPE member is an undergraduate student who acknowledges him or her as being committed to community service, longstanding pursuit for higher education, displaying outstanding organizational and leadership qualities, and is a highly dedicated member of the SHPE Familia.

The SHPE NJ Scholarship is established to provide a SHPE NJ member, who epitomizes the SHPE mission and fulfills our SHPE vision, with an opportunity to continue in their studies and remain actively involved with their SHPE chapter.

Requirements

- Must be a paid SHPE Member attending a college/university within NJ
- Undergraduate who is NOT graduating in May or Summer 2020
